# QA Lead Interview Collection

## Lesson 1 — QA Leadership Fundamentals

Description:

Learn how great QA Leaders create a quality culture, influence engineering teams, align quality with business goals, and lead without relying only on testing activities.

Learning Outcomes:

- Build quality ownership across the organization
- Influence engineering teams without direct authority
- Align quality goals with business priorities
- Create a sustainable quality culture
- Lead teams through organizational changes

Questions:

1. How do you build quality ownership beyond the QA team?

2. How do you influence quality without direct authority?

3. How do you build a culture of continuous quality improvement?

4. How do you improve collaboration between QA and Product?

5. How do you influence product teams to prioritize quality?

6. How do you improve quality culture across engineering?

7. How do you build trust with stakeholders during a crisis?

8. How do you handle conflicts between quality and delivery goals?
